EXAMPLE: Problem Statement (Mood Improver Bot Project)
Defined Problem (with data):Recent studies indicate that nearly 1 in 5 individuals (≈20%) experience frequent mood fluctuations or stress-related issues, especially among students and young adults. Additionally, over 60% of students report feeling overwhelmed due to academic pressure and digital fatigue, leading to reduced productivity and mental well-being.
Affected Users:
Students (school and college level) 
Young professionals 
Individuals experiencing daily stress, anxiety, or low motivation 
Need for Solution:There is a need for an accessible, personalized, and real-time digital solution that can help users regulate their mood through music recommendations, motivational content, and simple wellness interventions, without requiring professional intervention at all times.
